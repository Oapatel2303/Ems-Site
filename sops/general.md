# Seasons RP - EMS Standard Operating Procedures

## 1 | Disclaimer
> **The following text IS NOT a legal document and is purely for the use inside of Seasons Roleplay Server(s).** > We do request you to read and comply with the following SOPs to uphold the Seasons EMS standards.

## 2 | Introduction
Welcome to Seasons EMS SOP. We would like to thank you for coming onboard! SEMS (Seasons Emergency Medical Services) has two main goals: 
* Saving lives.
* Delivering the best possible care and aid towards the civilians of San Andreas.

We take pride in working as a team to reach the goal of achieving a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone! At SEMS, we don't hold nor have preferences towards anyone. All civilians of San Andreas are entitled to treatment equally.

## 3 | Bill of Rights For The Patient(s)
At SEMS we are committed to achieve and deliver the highest quality of health and care towards our patient(s). All patient(s) upon their discharge from our care or services have the right to:
* A full breakdown of their bill.
* Your Name & Callsign.

## 4 | Staff Code Of Conduct
At SEMS, we are here to deliver and achieve the highest quality of medical treatment while maintaining a professional and respecting outwards the patient(s), while also respecting the patient(s) rights.

## 5 | Patient(s) Billing Policy
You are required to attempt to bill patient(s) for the treatment you gave, while active PD members receive treatment for free.

## 6 | Ambulance and Equipment
All SEMS personnel should uphold the standard equipment that will be defined in training and other mandatory policies and must adhere to that said standard.

## 7 | Qualifications For SEMS Personnel
All members of SEMS need to be trained and given a basic care qualification before being allowed out on their own. This is to insure patient(s) receive the highest level of care while with us.

## 8 | Alerting Law Enforcement
While a large number of our patient(s) are friendly and supportive of SEMS, we do have to alert Law Enforcement to GSW (Gunshot Wound) victims and any patient(s) that attacks or threatens a member of SEMS. 
* GSW victims are to be treated and held in a secure room to await Law Enforcement.

## 9 | General Items

### Dignity & Respect
* All patient(s) have the right to respectful treatment (e.g., addressed by their preferred name).
* All patient(s) should be treated fairly regardless of:
  * Orientation
  * Race / Religion
  * Gender
  * Disability
* All SEMS personnel should **NOT** have bias based on anything for treatment.
* All patient(s) should be informed of their treatment they are going to receive/receiving, and SEMS personnel are to have consent from the patient(s) to perform the actions needed.

### Privacy & Doctor-Patient Confidentiality
* SEMS personnel are **NOT** to share any info from a patient(s) to 3rd parties unless the patient(s) have given WRITTEN consent and a Sr. FTO+ accepts.
* Patient(s) have the right to a private examination (Secure Rooms, etc.).

### Access to Care
* All patient(s) are entitled to timely treatment while under our care.
* Patient(s) should be triaged within 5-10 minutes of arrival.
* Patient(s) records must be released within 72 hours of WRITTEN consent and Sr. FTO approval.

### Quality of Care
* All SEMS personnel must hold updated and correct qualifications within their role.
* All SEMS personnel must ensure a safe environment with patient(s), maintaining professionalism while giving care.

### Consent and Advanced Directives
* All patient(s) must be informed of the procedure & risks involved with said procedure, and the patient(s) must understand and give verbal consent before patient care is given.

### Grievances and Appeals
* Filing a complaint should be done as soon as possible after the event/incident, and no later than 4 days.
* A complaint must be seen and investigated by HR within 48 hours of the event/incident being brought to attention.

### Financial Information
* All patient(s) are entitled to a clean and clear explanation of their care and their bill.
* An itemized bill statement should be shown within 4 days of said statement being asked for.

## Regulations & Notes

> **Vehicle Usage:** SEMS personnel are forbidden to use personal vehicles while on duty (unless extreme circumstances are met).

> **Power Gaming:** SEMS personnel are forbidden to change jobs to fit their needs on the spot.

> **Body Cams:** SEMS personnel aren't required to wear body cams but it is HIGHLY recommended. We suggest keeping clips for 4 days in case of complaints and incident reports.

> **Off-Duty Conduct:** SEMS personnel, when off duty, are not to use any EMS equipment (such as vehicles & medical equipment, etc.). This can be classed as whitelist abuse.

> **Internal Affairs:** SEMS Internal Affairs are to respond to all reports of misconduct regardless of if a clip is provided or not. All involved parties will be questioned by HR.

> **Vehicle SOPs:** SEMS personnel are to follow the EMS Vehicle SOP. You can find what you can and can't drive [here](https://1drv.ms/p/c/1b4af96293069d5d/EffWGzSkA_tNtGBqsi-p4x8BQtRlsRAfxBYkEL8Xr2n1HQ?e=ky2sZT).
