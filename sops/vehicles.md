# Seasons RP - EMS Vehicle SOPs
*Last Updated: 13/04/2026 by S. Bimbo*

> **Disciplinary Note:** Warnings and/or strikes will be given if these rules are not followed.

---

## Overall Vehicle Rules
* **01 | Personal Vehicles:** Absolutely no use of personal vehicles while in uniform. This includes using the Customization garage for your personal vehicle.
* **02 | Certifications:** Do not drive vehicles you are not certified to use. If you are interested in a certification, talk to the designated Department Director.
* **03 | Callsigns:** Use your callsign on top of your vehicle (Issi and Bike excluded).
* **04 | Parking & Security:** Always lock your vehicle and do not park it anywhere that endangers yourself or the public (ex. middle of the street).
* **05 | Emergency Lights & Sirens:** If you are responding to a call, use your lights and sirens. If you are not, lights and sirens should be off, and you should be driving safely and in accordance to city law.
* **06 | Customizations:** Customizations to your vehicle should be reasonable. None of the following are permitted: respray, underglow, unreasonable rim changes, spoilers, and anything else that would not be realistic for an EMS to drive.

### High-Speed & Tactical Units
**STX**
* **Max Speed:** ~140 mph | **Seats:** Four
* **Purpose:** To enable ultra-fast emergency response by deploying a high-performance EMS vehicle that allows paramedics to reach patients quickly and begin critical care ahead of ambulance arrival.
* **Authorization:** High Command, Tactical Response Cert and/or HSRS

**Issi**
* **Max Speed:** ~140 mph | **Seats:** Two
* **Purpose:** To provide rapid-response emergency care using a high-speed two-person unit, enabling faster arrival, immediate treatment, and efficient support for incoming ambulance crews.
* **Authorization:** Shift Lead+ and/or HSRS

**Speedo**
* **Max Speed:** ~145 mph | **Seats:** Seven
* **Purpose:** High-speed ambulances for rapid critical response, improving efficiency and patient outcomes in both urban and long-distance emergencies.
* **Authorization:** High Command, Tactical Response Cert and/or HSRS Cert
> **WARNING:** Speedo sirens and lights are bugged, this is not a recommended vehicle.

### Specialty Units
**Helicopter**
* **Max Speed:** Around 90+ mph | **Seats:** Four
* **Purpose:** To serve as a multi-mission aerial unit capable of search and rescue, on-scene triage, and high-priority medical evacuation under the most demanding conditions.
* **Authorization:** High Command, SAR (HALO Cert), Aviation Cert, Tactical Command (HALO Cert)

**Cara Cara**
* **Max Speed:** ~115 mph | **Seats:** Four
* **Purpose:** To support EMS operations in remote or hazardous environments by providing a specialized vehicle equipped for search, rescue, and on-site medical care.
* **Authorization:** High Command and/or SAR Cert

**Bike**
* **Max Speed:** ~145 mph | **Seats:** One
* **Purpose:** To enhance EMS efficiency by deploying motorcycle units for quick assessment, triage, and initial treatment, reducing response times and supporting ambulance crews.
* **Authorization:** High Command and/or HSRS (Motorcycle Cert)

**Wheelchair**
* **Max Speed:** ~25 mph | **Seats:** One
